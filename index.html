Why Traditional Thinktanks Fall Short

Five structural limitations that modern statecraft can no longer ignore.

  • Slow

    Analysis takes 6-18 months. By then, the world has moved on.

  • Fragmented

    Economics, Energy, and Geopolitics are analyzed in silos.

  • Static

    Reports are frozen in time. They don't update as data changes.

  • One-time

    High cost makes continuous strategic evaluation impossible.
